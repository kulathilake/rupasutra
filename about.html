Rupasutra.

Rupasutra was born out of a now dead passion for VFX, that begot a [strictly] conceptual VFX house with the same name, which was also supposed to build its own hypothetical yet cool tools, like the VFX house where blender's Tom Roosendal worked used to do.

Wanting to stick to the name so bad, decides to translate it differently. Derived from the two sanskrit words rupa and sutra meaning form (or image) and aphorism, Rupasutra roughly translates to Rules of Form. - that is after it was decided the early translation (Rules of [working with] Images) was too restrictive towards whatever it was to be done with the name.

What is left with a name so confusing and obviously holding no reference whatsoever to an ancient sex manual, is an archive, a portfolio, a product catalogue and a personal blog of a Software Engineer.
